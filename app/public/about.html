About the Portal

Preserving and practicing the rich musical traditions of Rathkeale College.

Our Mission

This portal was built to give students and staff instant access to our entire catalogue of school songs, house anthems, and chapel hymns. By providing dynamic learning features like full lyrics tracking, line-by-line breakdowns, and flashcards, we ensure that every member of our community can perform with confidence and pride.

Portal Rules & Regulations

To maintain the integrity of our cultural traditions and digital platform, all users must adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Respect and Reverence: Chapel hymns and traditional school anthems must be treated with appropriate respect during both independent practice and group assemblies.
  • Authorized Access Only: This portal is explicitly for the students and staff of Rathkeale College. Sharing platform access or media distribution pipelines outside the school network is prohibited.
  • Grooming & Presentation Expectations: Remember that practice leads to public presentation. All students are expected to uphold standard college grooming and uniform regulations during formal chapel services and house singing events where these tracks are performed.
  • Appropriate Request Behavior: When submitting requests or reporting issues, users must use their authentic full names and valid identification (Student ID or Staff Initials). Anonymized or spam submissions will be automatically purged.

🛠️ System Maintenance & Bug Reports

We are committed to keeping the multimedia engines, lyrics databases, and audio streams operating flawlessly. When an issue or missing track is submitted via the Contact page, it typically takes 1 to 3 days for the technical team to review, verify, and apply the fix to the live platform. We appreciate your patience as we maintain our database.
